InterPrior learns a scalable generative controller for humanoid human–object interactions.
It distills large-scale imitation into a goal-conditioned latent policy, then improves generalization via physics-based augmentation and RL finetuning. The resulting motion prior enables robust loco-manipulation under unseen goals and initializations.
